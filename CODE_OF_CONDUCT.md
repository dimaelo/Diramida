## Our Commitment

Diramida is committed to creating and maintaining a **respectful, inclusive, and collaborative community** where all members feel welcome and valued.

We welcome contributors and participants from all backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives‚Äîregardless of: 
- Age, body size, or visible disability
- Ethnicity, national origin, or immigration status
- Gender identity, gender expression, or sexual orientation
- Level of experience or expertise
- Neurodiversity or mental health status
- Perceived social or economic status
- Religion or lack thereof
- Political beliefs or affiliations

We pledge that everyone participating in this community will have a positive, harassment-free experience.

## Our Standards

### ‚úÖ Expected Behavior

We encourage all community members to: 

- **Be respectful** ‚Äî Value diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- **Be constructive** ‚Äî Provide helpful feedback focused on ideas, not persons
- **Be patient** ‚Äî Remember everyone is learning and developing
- **Be honest** ‚Äî Communicate authentically and transparently
- **Be inclusive** ‚Äî Actively welcome newcomers and different viewpoints
- **Be accountable** ‚Äî Take responsibility for your words and actions
- **Assume good intent** ‚Äî Interpret ambiguous messages charitably
- **Be collaborative** ‚Äî Work together toward shared goals

---

### ‚ùå Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated in any form:

- **Harassment** ‚Äî Unwanted comments, jokes, or behavior based on identity
- **Discrimination** ‚Äî Exclusion or unfair treatment based on protected characteristics
- **Intimidation or threats** ‚Äî Anything intended to frighten, coerce, or harm others
- **Trolling or insults** ‚Äî Personal attacks, name-calling, or deliberate provocation
- **Unwanted sexual attention** ‚Äî Advances, inappropriate comments, or unwelcome contact
- **Doxxing** ‚Äî Sharing personal information without consent
- **Spam** ‚Äî Unsolicited or repetitive messaging
- **Conspiracy theories** ‚Äî Promoting unverified claims or deliberate misinformation
- **Exclusionary behavior** ‚Äî Deliberately excluding people or silencing voices
- **Abuse of power** ‚Äî Misuse of moderation or administrative capabilities

## Reporting Issues

### How to Report

If you experience or witness a violation: 

1. **GitHub Issues** ‚Äî Create a private issue if available
2. **Discord Moderators** ‚Äî Direct message a moderator
3. **Email** ‚Äî Contact maintainers (available in repository)
4. **Trusted Intermediary** ‚Äî Reach out to someone you trust in the community

### What to Include

When reporting, provide:
- Description of the incident
- Who was involved (if comfortable sharing)
- When and where it occurred
- Witnesses or evidence (if available)
- What outcome or change you'd like to see

### Confidentiality & Safety

- Reports are handled confidentially
- Only relevant parties will know about the report
- We will not publicly disclose without explicit consent
- **Retaliation against reporters is strictly prohibited and subject to severe consequences**

---

## Our Response

### Investigation
- Reports are taken seriously and investigated promptly
- All parties have opportunity to respond
- We gather relevant information objectively
- Response time:  Initial contact within 7 days, resolution within 30 days

### Possible Actions

Depending on severity and context:

| Severity | Examples | Possible Actions |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| **Minor** | Thoughtless comments, misunderstandings | Private conversation, explanation, clear expectations |
| **Moderate** | Repeated low-level issues, unacceptable language | Formal warning, temporary channel suspension |
| **Serious** | Harassment, discrimination, threats | Suspension from community, removal from roles |
| **Critical** | Severe harassment, doxxing, illegal activity | Permanent ban, potential legal involvement |

### Transparency
- Parties involved are informed of outcomes
- Community safety updates may be posted (without identifying details)
- Decisions can be appealed through established process

## Support Resources

### If You Need Help

**Experiencing harassment or discrimination:**
- Document incidents if safe to do so
- Reach out to moderators or maintainers
- Consider whether you want to report formally

**Feeling unwelcome or excluded:**
- Ask for support in the community
- Connect with mentors or allies
- Reach out to leadership directly

**Accessibility or accommodation needs:**
- Contact maintainers about support options
- Interpretation/translation assistance available
- Accommodations considered on case-by-case basis

### General Support
- **Discord #support channel** ‚Äî Peer support and resources
- **Mentorship program** ‚Äî Pairing with experienced contributors
- **Community allies** ‚Äî Designated supporters for various backgrounds
