## Our Commitment

Diramida is committed to creating and maintaining a **respectful, inclusive, and collaborative community**. We welcome contributors, researchers, and participants from all backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives.

We pledge that everyone participating in this community will have a positive, harassment-free experience, regardless of: 
- Age, body size, or visible disability
- Ethnicity, national origin, or immigration status
- Gender identity, gender expression, or sexual orientation
- Level of experience, education, or expertise
- Neuroatypicality or mental health status
- Perceived social or economic status
- Religion or lack thereof
- Political beliefs or affiliations

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all spaces within the Diramida community, including:

- Discord server
- Telegram group
- Official meetings and events
- Video calls and streams
- Email and direct communication

## Reporting Issues

### How to Report

If you experience or witness a violation, please report it: 

1. **GitHub Issues** ‚Äî Create a private security issue (if available)
2. **Email** ‚Äî Contact maintainers directly (available in repository)
3. **Discord Moderators** ‚Äî DM a moderator in Discord
4. **Trusted Intermediary** ‚Äî Reach out to someone you trust in the community

### What to Include

When reporting, provide:
- Description of the incident
- Who was involved (if comfortable sharing)
- When and where it occurred
- Witnesses or evidence (if available)
- What outcome you'd like

### Confidentiality

- Reports will be handled confidentially
- Only relevant parties will know about the report
- We will not publicly disclose without consent
- Retaliation against reporters is not tolerated

---

## Our Response

### Investigation
- Reports are taken seriously and investigated promptly
- All parties will be given opportunity to respond
- We will gather relevant information
- Timeline:  Initial response within 7 days, resolution within 30 days

### Possible Outcomes

Depending on severity and context: 

| Severity | Possible Actions |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Minor** | Private conversation, warning, expectations clarified |
| **Moderate** | Formal warning, temporary suspension from channels |
| **Serious** | Suspension, removal, or banning from community |
| **Critical** | Immediate removal, potential legal involvement |

### Transparency
- Parties involved will be informed of outcomes
- Community safety updates may be posted (without identifying details)
- Decisions can be appealed through established process
